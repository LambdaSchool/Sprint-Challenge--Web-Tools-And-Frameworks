Lift Off

As we got further and further away, it [the Earth] diminished in size. Finally it shrank to the size of a marble, the most beautiful you can imagine, The beautiful, warm, living object looked so fragile, so delicate, that if you touched it with a finger it would fall aparet. Seeing this has to change a man because we realize how small & fragile our life really is. We are dust.
