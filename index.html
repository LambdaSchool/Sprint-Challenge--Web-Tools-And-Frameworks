Like most engines, rockets burn fuel. Most rocket engines turn the fuel into hot gas. The engine pushes the gas out its back. The gas makes the rocket move forward. you can see all of this happening when it blasts off.

The rocket can be seen for a while in the sky. until it is passed the atmosphere.

The rocket pushes on its exhaust. The exhaust pushes the rocket, too. The rocket pushes the exhaust backward. The exhaust makes the rocket move forward in launching.
