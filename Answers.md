4. Q: What are the names of the 5 breakpoints used in bootstrap?
   A: The 5 breakpoints in bootstrap are 'xs'(0-575.8px), 'sm'(576-767.8px), 'md'(768-991.8px), 'lg'(992-1199.8px), and 'xl'(1200+px).
